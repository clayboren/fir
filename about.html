About Us

Dr. Jason Haddock

Dr. Jason Haddock is a native of Florence, AL. He was raised in the Central community, and graduated from Central High School. He attended the University of North Alabama, where he received a Bachelor of Science in Biology. He then, continued his doctorate studies at Parker College of Chiropractic where he received his Doctorate of Chiropractic along with a Bachelor of Science in Anatomy. He has been in practice since 2004. Dr. Haddock believes in fully educating his patients on the benefits of Chiropractic treatment. He is sure to make his patients feel as comfortable as possible, and he spends as much time as needed to answer any questions that his patients may have. He believes that knowledge is power, and being educated on our musculoskeletal system is a vital key to maintaining great health. In his free time, he enjoys spending time with his family (wife, Katy and son, Jackson). He is an avid outdoorsman and enjoys fishing, hunting, camping and SEC football.

Rachel Karr-Brimer

Licensed Massage Therapist

Rachel Karr-Brimer is a full-time, licensed massage therapist, #2491, who works with clients to customize a massage experience for their needs whether it be for relaxation, managing chronic pain, or as part of a fitness regime. She has an associates degree in science from Bevill State Community College, has been nationally certified in therapeutic massage since 2007, is also certified by the Alabama board of massage therapy, and is a professional member of the American massage therapy association. She has been with First Choice Chiropractic for eight years. Rachel Karr-Brimer is skilled at both the Swedish massage, and the deep tissue massage. She believes in the physical and emotional benefits of massage therapy. She and her husband, Andrew, love the outdoors, traveling with family and friends, and going to the beach.

Willene Wright

Office Manager

Willene Wright has been in chiropractic 19 years, and worked for Dr. Jason Haddock for 9 years.  Willene specializes in insurance and billing. Having personally experienced the need for chiropractic care, she believes and knows first-hand that Chiropractic care is not only a form of pain relief, but it's also an important part of a healthy lifestyle. In Willene's spare time, she enjoys cooking, gardening, spending time with her grandson.

Amy Hendrix

Amy loves spending time with her husband, Randall, dog Jazzy, and cat Bullzeye. She also loves running and kayaking.
