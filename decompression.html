Spinal Decompression Therapy

Non-surgical Spinal Decompression Therapy is a non-drug, non-surgical, FDA approved treatment that helps reduce or eliminate painful herniated, degenerated, an bulging discs in the back and neck. The therapy is focused on decompressing the discs to reverse high amounts of pressure built up in the discs to take the pressure off of the nerves.

If you're having back pain, then please don't hesitate to call and make an appointment, so that we can give you the care you need today.
